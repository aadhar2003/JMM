I CONVERT CAMPAIGNS
INTO $$$$

I can make anything from a $2 sandpaper to a $200,000
robotic arm look irresistible. As an Assistant Marketing
Manager at JJS, an industrial importer of European
products, I'm constantly brainstorming creative
campaigns that will make people want to buy things they
didn't even know they needed.

Before joining JJS, I worked as a Marketing Coordinator
at Mosaic, where I helped over 25 small businesses
create a digital presence for their brands. Think of me
as a superhero with a knack for creating websites,
social media plans, and branding strategies that can
take any business to the next level.

But my marketing expertise wasn't just born overnight.
Before starting my career, I was pursuing my MSc from
Ivey Business School during the first COVID year of 2020.
Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, I was
determined to put my skills to good use, and I started
freelancing for small and medium business owners in the
US as a marketing specialist.

When I'm not busy coming up with the next big
marketing campaign, you can find me exploring new
restaurants in the city, practicing Bachata dancing, or
binge-watching the latest Netflix series.
